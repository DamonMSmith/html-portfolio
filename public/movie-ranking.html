The Best Movies According to Damon

My top movie series of all-time.

Star Wars

I grew up watching this with my Dad. Got to see most of the prequel and sequel trilogies in theaters.

Marvel

The Avengers are my favorite comic superheroes. I like most of the movies up to Infinity War/Endgame, although I am unfamiliar with many of the new characters introduced afterwards.

Lord of the Rings

A really cool epic fantasy between good and evil. Many Biblical parallels also. It's amazing how Tolkein developed an entire mythology just to write a children's story (The Hobbit)!
